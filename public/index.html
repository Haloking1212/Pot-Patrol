  • Wonders of your Report

    • Bypassed accidents

      When a driver tries to avoid that pothole, they may inadvertently drift into another lane, or even into oncoming traffic. On the other hand, hitting the pothole can cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle.

    • Bypassed expensive car repairs

      The pothole's rough surface can cause damage to both tires and rims, potentially leading to a blowout or other expensive repairs. Dipping into a pothole may also misalign your suspension or steering and these are just a few major scenarios that can happen.

    • Bypassed Injuries

      Common injuries include back injuries, head trauma, broken bones, and neck injuries. Such injuries typically require expensive treatment and extended recovery time.

  • Hitting a pothole is something everyone tries to avoid. Pot Patrol is here to improve your community, save someone’s life & wallet. Simply done by creating this site where people will send in reports of the potholes seen. Driver or pedestrian, take a minute, snap a picture and send in the report.
